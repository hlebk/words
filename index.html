Welcome!

What is Words?

It's a web application developed to assist you in memorizing new words from foreign languages. It's a convenient tool to practice and test your knowledge of vocabulary.

How does it work?

This app gives you freedom of preparing your own learning material. First, you have to create a text file (.txt) with words and their translations in a special format. Check How to use for specific instructions.
